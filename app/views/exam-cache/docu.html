Live Documentation Exercise

Minutes of the Meeting

Make for this week’s weekly meeting. Bring pen and paper. You are going to record its minutes. The format for documentation of the event is up to you, as long as it contains the following:

  • Time started and ended
  • Introduction
    The introduction should contain the objective of the meeting.
  • Attendance
    Attendance should include all those who were present, regardless of arrival time.
  • Agenda
    Separate each important point by placing agenda headers.
    State what decisions were made, what suggestions were rejected, and what points were up for contention.
* Submission of this documentation will be the day after the event, regardless of the 3 days deadline for this examination. Kindly coordinate to your Division Head for the schedule of the meeting.
